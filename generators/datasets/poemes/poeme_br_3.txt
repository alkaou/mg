**Matigi bɛ lafiɲɛbɔ min di i ma, i ka o diyabɔ.**
Aw tun ye dusukun jɔrɔlenw ye fɔlɔ laala,
Sugo fu ka tugu o kɔ;
Fili ye i tɔɔrɔ, walima koniya, walima keleya;
Aw daw, ɲɛnamaya lafiɲɛ bɔra minnu na,
U tun falen bɛ mankan na.
Ɲɛda minnu bɛ u yɛrɛ jira ɲɔgɔn na ni ɲagami ye,
Aw nana ka taa jamaba la nbɛdaw la.
Ka dabila,
Jɔrɔnanko i n'a fɔ ji min bɛ woyo jibolisiraw la,
Bɛɛ, ka taama k’a sɔrɔ u ma kɛ cogo si la, ka dimi kelenw sɔrɔ,
O fɛɛrɛ kelenw ɲagamini.
N’a sɔrɔ tasuma tun bɛ ka jeni i kunkolo jenilen na,
Porozɛw, jigiya, kari cɛ Élysée,
Cɛ min bɔra Vatikan,
Aw ka hɔrɔnya hakili bɔn dugukolo kan ni jikurubaw ye;
Sabula nin san kɛmɛ tasumaman in na, ni bɛɛ ye kɔba ye
Wa bɛɛ sigilen bɛ tasuma-fanga dɔ la.
I ye kanu, i dusukun sirilen don ni cakɛdaw ye,
Wula fɛ, i y'a ye ko i y'i yɛrɛ di siran fuw ma.
A falen bɛ haminankow la minnu bɛ mɔgɔ dusu minɛ,
Kɔgɔji b’a ye k’a ka jikuruw bɛ ka wuli tuma min na,
Wuli i kɔnɔ jikuru ba kelen jugumanba
Sankolo kɛnɛman jukɔrɔ.
Bɛɛ, i tun ye mɔgɔ o mɔgɔ ye, kuntigi fariman, hakili hakilitigi,
Ni funankɛya bɛ yeelen bɔ i ɲɛ na wo, walima kɔrɔya mana kɛ cogo o cogo
ye i minɛ k' i biri.
A kɛra siniko ye i bolo, a kɛra dusukasi ye wo, a kɛra gundo ye wo, a kɛra seli ye wo,
Kanuya tun bɛ aw dusukunw na, nin fɔɲɔba in,
Dimi, nin kɛlɛ in.
Barika la desanburukalo tile naaninan ye, bi, k’a sɔrɔ miirili ma kɛ,
I dalen bɛ i kɔrɔtalen dingɛ kɔnɔ min falen bɛ nɛnɛ na
O finimugu belebelebaw jukɔrɔ;
E suw, bin mankan tɛ min na, o bɛ falen i ka katakɔnw kan,
Aw ka sunɔgɔ aw ka sanubɔlanw kɔnɔ! aw ka aw makun aw kaburuw kɔnɔ !
Mansamara ye hɛrɛ ye.

Jules Breton :
Dugukolo, n’a kununna, a ɲɛ ye binkɛnɛ ye ani a ɲiginnen don,
Dugu lamini min datugulen don ni bulu belebelebaw ye
Sunɔgɔ so sanfɛlanw bɛ sisi yɔrɔ min na ni dususuma ye;
Siliki foli kɛnɛba kɔnɔ, foyi tɛ min lamini,
Lenburuba azure, lenburuba ani alfalfa cɛma,
A bɛ malo denmisɛnninw fara ɲɔgɔn kan minnu falen bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ la.

A lafiyalen don ani a bɛ lafiya ni sisi dilalenw ye,
U ka nɔnɔ siraw susuli nɔgɔlen jukɔrɔ,
A bɛ iko a bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ ɲɛji dɔlɔmin na,
Wa, a lamininen ni nafolo ye, a fan belebeleba falen bɛ minnu na,
I ka nisɔndiya cɛ banbali la min b'a balo,
Tile jɛlen min bɛ bɔ, a bɛ ɲɛnamaya, ka bɔ falenfɛnw dilan na.

Dolow vermilion bɛ woyo a ka fiyɛli la min bɛ mɔgɔ ɲɛnafin bɔ;
Fula kelen-kelen bɛɛ, u bɛ sɛgɛn makɔnɔni languor la,
Voluptuously, ka taa tile tasuma-yɔrɔ fan fɛ
A b'a jiribolo wuli k'a ka jifilen kɔrɔtalen kɔrɔta,
Ani minfɛnw i ka charme la, Aurore, ani blushes ni nisɔndiya ye...
Wa, banakisɛ bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ kanuya yeelen dumanw na.

Dusukunnata jumɛn ka di ka tɛmɛ kanuya kan? Nin dusukunnata in min bɛ maga an na an yɛrɛ kɔnɔ kosɛbɛ, nin fanga in ani nin nege in min b’an bila ka taa mɔgɔ wɛrɛw fɛ ani an tɛ se ka min ɲɛfɔ... A ka gɛlɛn tuma dɔw la ka daɲɛw sɔrɔ walasa k’an dusukunnataw fɔ an dusukun sugandilen ye o la mun na an tɛ ci a ka kanuya poyi dɔɔnin? Poyikɛlaw bɛ nin nilifɛn in sɔrɔ k’a dɔn kanuya kumacogo dɔnni i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ wɛrɛ. O la sa, n’i ye kanuya ye i yɛrɛ la, n’i b’a fɛ k’a fɔ a ye ko i b’a kanu ni o kuma cɛɲiw ye, misali damadɔw filɛ nin ye. Kanuya poyi a ye: poyi 12 minnu bɛna maga a dusukun na.
